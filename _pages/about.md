Welcome to my website!
I am a PhD student at ZEW – Leibniz Centre for European Economic Research in Mannheim, where I work in the Research Unit of Environmental and Climate Economics under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Sebastian Rausch. My research lies at the intersection of environmental economics, innovation economics, and energy policy. I study how market structure and competition shape the pace and direction of technological change, with a focus on electrification and the clean energy transition.
